Policy Cancellations only

Important Information
If you wish to Cancel your Policy, you are eligible for a refund of your premium within the 15 day cooling off period from the Policy Issuance date only, subject to the conditions that you have not, and do not intend to make a claim against this policy and your trip has not started. Refunds outside of the cooling off period will not be provided.

You may cancel by any of the methods below:

1. Online – Log on to the travel website with your itinerary number, and click on the policy cancellation link. Your refund request will be acknowledged and confirmed instantly! 
2. By phone – Please contact the travel website using the contact information provided on their itinerary email. Your refund request will be acknowledged and processed during standard business hours, excluding public holidays.

Refund processing time: Your refund may take up to thirty (30) business days to reflect on your payment method and up to 2 billing cycles to show on your bank statement. For enquiries on when you will receive your refund, it is best to contact your financial institution directly.
