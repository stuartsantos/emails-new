ELIGIBILITY REMINDERS:
  • This policy offers coverage only to Canadian Residents travelling outside of their Home.
  • Please take note of the maximum age limits for eligibility for cover per product:
    1. All Insured Persons must be under the age of 76 years old on the date of Trip departure to be eligible for cover under the Cancellation Plan product.
    2. All Insured Persons must be under the age of 66 years old on the date of Trip departure to be eligible for cover under the Protection Plan product.
    3. For the Collision Damage Plan, Insured Persons must be aged 25 – 70 years to be eligible for coverage.
  • Hotel only bookings: For customers purchasing insurance alongside a Hotel only booking, this insurance is for the period from leaving their Home or usual place of stay in their Country of Departure (up to 24 hours before their scheduled check-in time), until their return Home or to their usual place of stay in their Country of Departure up to 24 hours after their scheduled check-out time from their Expedia booked accommodation.
