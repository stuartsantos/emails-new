We know that travel can be unpredictable, and sometimes the unexpected can happen. If you need to lodge a claim you can do this online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Claims can be lodged from overseas or when you return home to New Zealand. Simply lodge your claim online via the Claims Portal.

Claims Portal

To ensure that your claim can be assessed by our team as quickly as possible, make sure that you include all of the required documents with your completed claim form. These can include:

  • Original invoices or receipts related to any expenses you incurred that relate to your claim.
  • Supporting documentation from your travel providers including airlines, hotels, or other booking companies. If you have been provided with a refund or credit from your travel providers then you will not be able to claim for that refund or credited amount.
  • Medical reports, if you have incurred medical or hospital costs and expenses whilst overseas and you need to claim for these costs and expenses.

Once your claim has been submitted and acknowledged, our claims team will start the assessment process. Please allow up to 10 business days from the day you submitted your claim for our team to assess and provide you with a response.
