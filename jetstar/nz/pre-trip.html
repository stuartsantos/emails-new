It’s not long now until your trip with Jetstar begins! So that your trip is worry free and runs smoothly, there are a few things we want to remind you about:

Before you travel, don’t forget to:

  • Read your Policy Wording to be clear on what is and isn’t covered under your Jetstar Travel Insurance policy.
  • Research the area(s) you are travelling to and read the latest travel alerts, government advice and entry requirements at your destination.
  • Print a copy of your travel insurance documents. You can also easily access this information on the View Policy Portal.
  • Save a copy on your phone of your itinerary, receipts and other important documents like a copy of your passport and booking confirmations or print them and take them with you.

More than ever, it is important that you stay up to date and are informed about your destination(s) before you leave and while you are travelling. As your insurance may be impacted, you should visit the SafeTravel website to research the destinations(s) you are travelling to, check for any restrictions, government and health advice, including vaccination requirements or recommendations.
