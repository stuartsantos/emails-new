# Jetstar AU Revision Documents

This folder contains revision documents for the Jetstar AU email templates.

## File Mapping

| Revision Document | Email Template |
|---|---|
| `jetstar-au-cancel.docx` | [cancel.html](../en/cancel.html) |
| `jetstar-au-post-trip.docx` | [post-trip.html](../en/post-trip.html) |
| `jetstar-au-pre-trip.docx` | [pre-trip.html](../en/pre-trip.html) |
| `JS AU Policy Confirmation Email.docx` | [policy-confirmation.html](../en/policy-confirmation.html) |

## Naming Convention

- Most revision documents follow the pattern: `jetstar-au-[template-name].docx`
- The corresponding email template is located at: `../en/[template-name].html`
