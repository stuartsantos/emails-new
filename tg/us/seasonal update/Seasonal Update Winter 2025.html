SEASONAL UPDATE
Winter Weather 2025
Winter is on its way and experts are predicting an intense and stormy winter for many areas of the country1. Whether you’re someone who enjoys a winter wonderland or wants to escape to somewhere warm, having the right travel insurance plan with you can make all the difference.

As much fun as winter weather can be, there’s no denying your travel plans could very well be affected by severe winter weather like blizzards, ice storms and freezing temperatures. Travel Guard provides travel insurance plans with coverages designed to make the most of your trip. Our Trip Saver and Trip Exchange coverage can help save your vacation and our 24/7 travel emergency assistance services team can help you when severe weather threatens to throw you off track.

And remember, your travel plans can be impacted by weather even if winter storms aren’t happening in your immediate location or destination. Cancelled flights and rebooking travels can create a domino effect at the airport causing delays across the board.
