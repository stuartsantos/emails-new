Seasonal Update

Cruise Season

Cruising has become increasingly popular over the past decade. In 2024 a total of 35.7 million passengers1 set sail. That's four million more passengers than 2023. If you're one of the many travelers looking to book a cruise vacation, now's the perfect time to look for packages that suit you. Why? It's wave season – from January to March cruise lines typically offer special discounts and deals for cruises throughout the year.

Whether you're hoping to tour around the Caribbean or set sail on a European river cruise, make sure you have the travel insurance coverage you need for your trip. Comprehensive travel insurance plans from Travel Guard can include benefits like Baggage coverage, emergency medical expense coverage, trip cancellation and interruption coverage and more. With Travel Guard's unique Travel Inconvenience Benefit there is even a benefit that provides a flat payment for cruise diversion if your ship doesn't stop at a scheduled port of call! Plus, all Travel Guard plans include 24/7 access to our Emergency Services Team to help you handle the unexpected.

Travel with confidence on your cruise vacation with Travel Guard.
