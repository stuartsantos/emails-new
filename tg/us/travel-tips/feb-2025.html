Money Saving Travel Tips
Travel is a very fulfilling experience for many, but if you’re not financially prepared, it can put a strain on your wallet. Thankfully, there are plenty of ways you can save money while still enjoying your vacation to the fullest. Here are our tips to help you create a budget-friendly plan for your next trip:

Look for Deals
When booking your stay, be sure to look outside of popular cities to help find lower prices on hotels and lodging. If flying, you may want to consider signing up for notifications on cheap flights, including mistake fares, so you don’t miss out on inexpensive airline tickets. It can be helpful if you’re flexible with your travel dates to find the best deals on flights and hotels. Additionally, traveling during off season rather than peak season is a great way to find discounted rates.

Explore Free Activities
That’s right. There are plenty of fun, free activities you can enjoy while on vacation. What savvy traveler doesn’t love gaining new experiences for free? Try things like exploring a museum with free entry, joining a walking tour to sightsee, visiting historic grounds, attending a cultural festival, hiking various trails or relaxing on the beach. You don’t need to spend a lot of money to make the most of your travel experience.

Research Transportation
Don’t be afraid to embrace public transportation. In many locations, trains or subways are more affordable than taxis. Some cities have public transportation cards that you can pre-load and refill throughout your trip. Larger cities usually have unlimited use passes that are good for three to seven days allowing you to ride public transportation as often as you’d like over that time, which is especially helpful if you plan to do a lot of sightseeing or have an itinerary full of activities. If your travel plans have you exploring multiple cities, renting a car may be a more efficient and cost-effective option. If you choose this route, Travel Guard offers Rental Car Insurance Plans to help protect your journey. However you decide to get around on your budget-friendly vacation, it’s worth researching costs prior to arriving at your destination.

While the cost of travel can seem intimidating, you can create an amazing vacation without exceeding your budget. When you’re ready for your next vacation, remember Travel Guard can help protect your travel investment along the way.
