Tips for Avoiding the Airport Rush
You’re likely no stranger to the overwhelming experience at the airport, which often makes this part of the trip seem daunting before even the most exciting trips. Thankfully, there are several ways travelers can simplify the check-in process at the airport. Discover our top tips for navigating the airport like an expert!

1. Take advantage of online check-in
Passengers have more control over the check-in process than ever before. One reason for this is the ability to check-in early through your mobile device. Before arriving to the airport, be sure to download the app from the airline you’re using. Using this app allows your information to be easily accessible even without Wi-Fi. Additionally, if you need luggage checked, some airlines will allow you to check your bags online then simply drop them off when you arrive for your flight.

2. Know the baggage policies
Baggage fees for domestic flights can cost up to $200, while international passengers can see fees up to $350. You can avoid the bag check process all together by traveling with carry-on luggage only. Almost every airline allows one carry-on suitcase, stored in the overhead compartment and one personal item that must fit under the seat in front of you.

3. Apply for TSA PreCheck® or Global Entry
TSA PreCheck lets domestic travelers to get access to accelerated security screening benefits. Travelers can apply for a five-year membership for a fee of $85. For international travelers, Global Entry provides faster customs screenings. Global Entry has a $100 application fee with a membership of five years, which is worthwhile if you are a frequent traveler. Global Entry benefits include access to TSA PreCheck in addition to the expedited customs screening, so consider carefully when deciding which program you’d prefer.

4. Consider subscribing to CLEAR®
Cut down on your wait at airport security by subscribing to CLEAR, a program for registered travelers that allows members to quickly verify their identity through touchless, biometric security kiosks. As an added bonus, CLEAR can be used in conjunction with TSA PreCheck, allowing passengers with both benefits to skip even the PreCheck security line and proceed straight to the security screening point. However, CLEAR is not available at every airport, so be sure to check before your flight. For more information on CLEAR, visit their FAQ page.
