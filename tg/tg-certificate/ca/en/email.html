ELIGIBILITY REMINDERS:

  • All insured persons must be residents of Canada.
  • Please take note of the maximum age limits for eligibility for cover per product:
    1. All insured persons must be under the age of 76 years old on the date of trip departure to be eligible for cover under the Cancellation Plan, Basic Trip Protection, Trip Protection Plus, Hotel Cancellation, Hotel Cancellation Plus and Hotel Interruption products. 
    2. All insured persons must be under the age of 66 years old on the date of trip departure to be eligible for cover under the Protection Plan, Premier Trip Protection, Hotel Interruption & Medical, Hotel Comprehensive Basic and Hotel Comprehensive Plus products. 
    3. For the Collision Damage Plan, drivers must be aged 25 – 70 years to be eligible for coverage.
  • Protection Plan, Premier Trip Protection, Hotel Interruption & Medical, Hotel Comprehensive Basic and Hotel Comprehensive Plus provide cover of up to C$ 262,000 in total for both B1- Emergency Medical Expenses and B2 – Emergency Medical Evacuation & Repatriation if an insured person is diagnosed with COVID-19 during their travels.
  • Hotel only bookings: For customers purchasing insurance alongside a Hotel only booking, this insurance is for the period from leaving their Home or usual place of stay in their Country of Departure (up to 24 hours before their scheduled check-in time), until their return Home or to their usual place of stay in their Country of Departure up to 24 hours after their scheduled check-out time from their Expedia booked accommodation .
